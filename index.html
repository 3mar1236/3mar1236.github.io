Who We Are

Migraine Canada™ is only national, volunteer-based charity focused on supporting all Canadians impacted by migraine and other headache conditions.

We are transforming the lives of people affected by migraine and headache conditions through awareness campaigns, education and support programs, advocacy and research.

Mission

To improve the lives of Canadians with migraine and other headache disorders through advocacy, awareness, education, research and support.

Vision

Canadians living with migraine and headache disorders are diagnosed, treated and supported so their quality of life is optimized.
